How to Use Kohl's Credit Card for Curbside Pickup

  • Home
  • Blog
  • Blog Details

In today’s fast-paced world, convenience and safety are top priorities for shoppers. With the rise of contactless services, curbside pickup has become a game-changer for retail. Kohl’s, a leading department store in the U.S., offers this service to make shopping easier—especially when paired with the Kohl’s Credit Card. Whether you’re avoiding crowded stores or simply saving time, here’s how to maximize your Kohl’s Credit Card benefits for curbside pickup.

Why Kohl’s Curbside Pickup is a Must-Try

Curbside pickup isn’t just a trend; it’s a reflection of how retail is evolving. Here’s why it’s worth using:

  • Time-Saving: Skip the lines and get your items delivered to your car.
  • Contactless: Perfect for health-conscious shoppers.
  • Eco-Friendly: Reduces the need for shipping packaging.

Pairing this service with your Kohl’s Credit Card unlocks even more perks, like cashback rewards and exclusive discounts.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Kohl’s Credit Card for Curbside Pickup

1. Place Your Order Online or via the Kohl’s App

Start by browsing Kohl’s website or mobile app. Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout.

  • Pro Tip: Use your Kohl’s Credit Card at checkout to earn Kohl’s Cash or reward points.
  • Hot Deal Alert: Check for cardholder-exclusive promotions before finalizing your purchase.

2. Select Curbside Pickup at Checkout

During checkout, choose “Pick Up In-Store” and then select “Curbside Pickup” as your delivery method.

  • Note: Not all items are eligible for curbside pickup (e.g., large furniture may require in-store pickup).
  • Double-Check: Ensure your local Kohl’s store offers curbside service.

3. Wait for Your “Ready for Pickup” Notification

Kohl’s will send an email or app notification when your order is ready—usually within a few hours.

  • Speed It Up: Opt for express pickup if available.
  • Keep Your Card Handy: You’ll need it for verification upon arrival.

4. Drive to the Store and Check In

Once you arrive:

  • Park in a designated curbside pickup spot.
  • Use the Kohl’s app or call the store number on the parking sign to check in.

5. Show Your Kohl’s Credit Card for Verification

A Kohl’s associate will bring your order to your car. To confirm your identity:

  • Present your Kohl’s Credit Card (or the last 4 digits).
  • Show a photo ID if requested.

6. Enjoy Your Purchase—and Your Rewards!

After receiving your items, check your Kohl’s Credit Card account for:

  • Earned Kohl’s Cash (if applicable).
  • Reward Points toward future purchases.

Maximizing Your Kohl’s Credit Card Benefits

Earn More with Every Purchase

The Kohl’s Credit Card offers:

  • 5% Rewards in Kohl’s Cash on most purchases.
  • Special Financing on large purchases (e.g., 12 months no interest).

Stack Discounts Like a Pro

Combine curbside pickup with:

  • Kohl’s Coupons (cardholders often get extra discounts).
  • Seasonal Sales (Black Friday, Cyber Monday, etc.).

Stay Secure with Contactless Payments

Since curbside pickup minimizes physical interaction, using your Kohl’s Credit Card adds an extra layer of security—no cash handling required.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

“My Order Isn’t Ready Yet”

  • Check your email for delays.
  • Call the store if it’s past the estimated ready time.

“I Forgot My Kohl’s Credit Card”

  • Use the Kohl’s app to access your digital card.
  • Bring a photo ID as backup.

“The App Won’t Let Me Check In”

  • Restart the app or call the store directly.
  • Ensure location services are enabled.

The Future of Retail: Why Kohl’s is Leading the Way

Kohl’s has embraced digital transformation by integrating curbside pickup with its credit card rewards system. This aligns with global shifts toward:

  • Sustainable Shopping (less packaging waste).
  • Faster Fulfillment (competing with Amazon and Walmart).
  • Personalized Offers (AI-driven discounts for cardholders).

By leveraging your Kohl’s Credit Card for curbside pickup, you’re not just shopping smarter—you’re part of a retail revolution.

So next time you’re eyeing that must-have deal, remember: a few taps on your phone and a quick curbside stop could save you time, money, and stress. Happy shopping!
